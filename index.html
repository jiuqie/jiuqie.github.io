I am a third-year master student (expected to graduate in June 2024) in Academy for Advanced Interdisciplinary Studies, Peking University, majoring in data science. Previously I was an undergraduate stundent in School of Mathmatical Science, Peking University, majoring in statistics.

Research interests

I mainly work in computational biology and bioinformatics using tools from statistics, machine learning and computational mathematics. I am also interested in high-dimensional statistics and theoretical analysis of deep learning.

My previous research projects include:

  • Mathematical modeling for single-cell sequencing techniques.
  • Algorithmic development and theoretical analysis of RNA velocity in cell development using stochastic analysis.
  • Theoretical analysis of invariant risk minimization and generability in deep learning.
  • De-noising of count matrix data in biological applications.

Keywords: Computational Biology, Statistics, Stochastic Analysis, Deep Learning.
